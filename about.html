About me

Guiding Your Path to Growth

I am an experienced and accredited life coach with a background in holistic wellness and healing. My journey in personal development and coaching began with a passion for helping women overcome challenges and rediscover their inner strength. Over the years, I have supported individuals in navigating life's transitions, healing emotional wounds, and finding clarity in their purpose.

At Sasa Evans Coaching, my goal is to help you renew, reframe, and refocus your mindset so you can overcome life’s challenges, reclaim your inner strength, and create a life filled with joy and purpose. Whether you're feeling stuck, unfulfilled, or uncertain about your next steps, I am here to guide and support you with compassion and inspiration.
